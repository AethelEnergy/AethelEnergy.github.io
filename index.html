How It Works

Electricity delivered
via the national grid

Wheeling is the mechanism that allows renewable energy generated at a remote solar or wind facility to be transmitted across Eskom's distribution network directly to your business premises — no on-site panels required.

  • The generator produces clean energy at a licensed renewable facility
  • Power flows through the existing Eskom grid infrastructure (the "wire")
  • Your business receives that energy at an agreed, fixed PPA tariff
  • You pay Eskom a wheeling charge for use of the network — but your net cost is still below standard tariff
  • Aethel Energy originate, structures, and manages the entire agreement on your behalf
